To find the value of x in the equation 3x + 7 = 16, we need to isolate x on one side of the equation. 

First, we subtract 7 from both sides of the equation to get:

3x + 7 - 7 = 16 - 7
3x = 9

Next, we divide both sides by 3 to solve for x:

3x/3 = 9/3
x = 3

Therefore, the value of x in the equation 3x + 7 = 16 is x = 3.
